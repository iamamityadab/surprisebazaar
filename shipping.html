Shipping Policy

Learn about our shipping processes, timeframes, and coverage.

1. Shipping Coverage

We ship to most locations across India. For remote locations, delivery might take longer and may be subject to availability.

2. Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. During high-demand periods, processing may take an additional 1-2 days.

3. Shipping Charges

Shipping is free for orders above Rs. 499. For orders below that amount, a nominal shipping fee of Rs. 49 is applicable.

4. Delivery Time

Standard delivery time is 3-7 business days based on your location. You will receive tracking information once the product is shipped.

5. Tracking Orders

After your order is shipped, a tracking link will be sent to your registered email and mobile number. You can also track your order from your account dashboard.

6. Undeliverable Packages

If the package is returned to us due to an incorrect address or failed delivery attempts, we will contact you to arrange re-shipping at an additional charge.

7. Delays Due to External Factors

We are not liable for delays caused by natural disasters, strikes, lockdowns, or courier issues. We strive to deliver as soon as possible in such scenarios.

8. Damaged or Lost Shipments

In case your shipment is damaged or lost during transit, please contact our support within 48 hours of delivery for resolution or refund.

9. International Shipping

Currently, we only offer shipping within India. International shipping is not supported at this time.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions about your shipment, please reach out to our support team at support@surprisebazaar.com or call +91-XXXXXXXXXX.
