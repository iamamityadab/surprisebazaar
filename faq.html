Frequently Asked Questions

Your doubts, our answers.

1. What is Surprise Bazaar?

Surprise Bazaar is an online marketplace offering surprise deals on electronics, fashion, gadgets, and more.

2. How can I track my order?

Login to your account and visit the 'My Orders' section to track your shipments.

3. Do you offer cash on delivery?

Yes, we offer COD on selected products and locations.

4. What is the return policy?

You can return products within 7 days of delivery. Please read our Return Policy for more details.

5. How do I cancel an order?

Go to 'My Orders' and click on 'Cancel' next to the order you'd like to cancel before it's shipped.

6. Are there any hidden charges?

No hidden charges. All prices are inclusive of taxes unless stated otherwise.

7. How can I contact customer support?

Email us at support@surprisebazaar.com or visit the Contact Us page.

8. Is it safe to shop from Surprise Bazaar?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard security protocols and encrypted payments.

9. Can I change my shipping address?

Yes, before shipment. Go to your account settings or contact support.

10. Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we deliver across India only. International shipping will be introduced soon.
