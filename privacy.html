Privacy Policy

We value your trust. Here's how we protect it.

1. What information do we collect?

We collect your name, email address, shipping address, phone number, and payment details during purchase or account registration.

2. How do we use your information?

We use your data to process orders, improve user experience, send order updates, and occasionally share promotional content.

3. Do we share your data?

We do not sell your personal information. We only share with trusted partners for delivery and payment processing.

4. How is my data protected?

We implement security protocols including SSL encryption, secure servers, and strict access controls to protect your data.

5. Can I update or delete my information?

Yes, you can manage your account info anytime. Contact support@surprisebazaar.com for deletion requests.

6. What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device to enhance browsing experience. You can disable them via browser settings.

7. Third-party services

We may use third-party tools like Google Analytics to understand visitor behavior. Their privacy policies may apply.

8. Changes to policy

We may update our privacy policy. Changes will be notified via email or a notice on our homepage.

9. Children's privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If identified, such data will be deleted.

10. Contact Us

Have questions? Reach out at privacy@surprisebazaar.com.
